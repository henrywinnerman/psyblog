Learn Physics?

Learn Physics is what you can call an online physics handbook.

Physics is divided into five categpries which are: Mechanics, Heat, Electricity, Light, Optics and Waves. This handbook is designed to cover these five aspects of Physics and improve your understanding of each subject matter.

Practice Questions

The Practice questions are designed to help you test your understanding of each topic. Your scores will be revealed at the end of each test and you will also get to see the correct answers to the questions.

Practicals

The practical aspect contains certain informationn on how to solve practical questions ranging from how to determine scale for graphs, how to determine slope, how to determine intercept, writing safety precautions etc.
