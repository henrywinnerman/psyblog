Work, Energy and Power

Work

Work is the product of force and displacement. A work is said to be done if the force is channeled in the direction of a displacement. Thus, Force applied without a partiular dicplacement cannot be qualified as work done. A boy carrying a log of wood in a particular place for hours has done no work, his action can only be described as work if he covers a particular distance while carrying the log of wood.

Mathematically, Work = Force x Distance.

Meanwhile, Force = mass x acceleration , in this case the acceleration is due to gravity, so, we'll say : F= mxg

F = mg, Work = mg x Distance

The S.I Unit of workdone is Joules.

Energy

Energy is the ability to do work. Energy can neither be created nor destroyed, but it cam change from one form to another.

Energy exists in different forms, some of which are: Mechanical Energy (sub divided into : Potential and Kinetic Energy), Electrical Energy, Sound Energy, Heat Energy, Light Energy etc
